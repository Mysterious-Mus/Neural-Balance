\section{Related Work}
\label{sec:related-work}

This section situates the compared regularization approaches within the broader literature. At minimum, it should cover:
\begin{itemize}
  \item classical regularization (weight decay, dropout),
  \item implicit regularization effects of optimization,
  \item re-parameterization and scale-invariance phenomena in deep nets,
  \item synaptic neural balance and closely related balancing or rescaling methods \citep{alexos2025synapticbalance}.
\end{itemize}
