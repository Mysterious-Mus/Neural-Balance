\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

This report compared several regularization approaches for neural networks, with a particular focus on synaptic neural balance. The results and analysis in Sections~\ref{sec:evaluation} and~\ref{sec:discussion} summarize when classical techniques suffice and when balancing-based procedures provide measurable benefits.

Future work includes expanding to additional architectures and datasets, exploring systematic combinations of balancing with standard regularizers, and better characterizing the regimes where balancing is most effective.
